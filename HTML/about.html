About CapiLocator

CapiLocator is your travel and relocation buddy, designed to help you locate capital cities around the world and compare the cost of living in these cities. CapiLocator provides data on various expenses, including food, housing, transportation, healthcare, and more, to help you make informed decisions about your travel or relocation plans. The waterfall development approach was used develop the website. The development stack is HTML5, CSS, jQuery, Bootstrap 5, and Chart.js. The data on the website are gotten from Rapid API, Rest Countries API, and API Ninja. All the pictures were downloaded from Unsplash.
